\chapter*{Abstract}
This Thesis Work focuses on investigating the role of the environment in the mass accretion of a SMBH, through a comparative analysis made using samples of BCGs and Non-BCGS. %Migliorabile seguendo i commenti che mi ha fatto Andrea
We have been able to make this study by inspecting a galaxy sample made by the team MPA-JHU on the reference of Raw data of Data Release 7 in the context of the project "The Sloan Digital Sky Survey".
